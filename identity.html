02 / Identity

One identity. Four useful forms.

Original signal aperture concept: an engineered A, an edge rail and a live node — with the ACTIVE ESL wordmark.

Rules

  • Never stretch, skew, condense, redraw or rearrange a lockup
  • Clear space of at least one central-node width
  • Symbol minimum 24 px digital; horizontal 140 px; print lockup from 28 mm wide
  • Below minimum size, use the symbol only and test the fabrication process
  • Never brand a product as “Electric Edge”

Line

Embedded intelligence. Made real.

Use for heroes and stakeholder materials. Keep legal entity naming for contracts and formal documents.
