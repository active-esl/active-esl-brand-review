Proposed product range

One family. Eight targeted boards.

Each board is designed as a single-board solution: evaluate quickly, then manufacture the same design in volume. Where a customer needs a different form factor or I/O set, we customise the hardware and firmware through consultancy.

HANDHELD

EDGE TABLET

i.MX 8M Mini

10.1" multitouch tablet with dual cameras, Wi-Fi 6 tri-radio and USB-C / Qi charging for field and care workflows.

HANDHELD PRO

EDGE FOLD PRO

i.MX 8M Plus · 2.3 TOPS

13" AMOLED foldable-class handheld with stronger NPU, USB 3 and higher-resolution cameras for premium HMI use.

COMPUTE

EDGE PI

i.MX 8M Mini

Raspberry Pi–compatible industrial compute board with 40-pin header, Mini HDMI and USB Type-A for rapid migration from prototypes.

COMPUTE PRO

EDGE PI PRO

i.MX 8M Plus · 2.3 TOPS

Pro Pi-compatible platform with USB 3, neural acceleration and dual camera interfaces for higher-performance designs.

LOW POWER

EDGE LP

i.MX 93 · 0.5 TOPS

Battery-focused low-power board for always-available edge AI, sensing and scheduled cloud updates.

BASELINE

EDGE LITE

i.MX 8M Mini

Compact baseline board for cost-sensitive connected products that still need industrial temperature support.

GATEWAY

EDGE GATEWAY

i.MX 8M Mini

Communications gateway with dual Gigabit Ethernet, M.2 LTE, mPCIe radio expansion and PoE+ options.

GATEWAY PRO

EDGE GATEWAY PRO

i.MX 8M Plus

Pro gateway with TSN Ethernet, 5G-capable M.2 expansion and the headroom for denser edge workloads.
