Privacy

How we handle your information.

Active-Edge Solutions Ltd is responsible for personal information supplied when you arrange or take part in a product-platform consultation.

Who we are

Active-ESL is the customer-facing brand of Active-Edge Solutions Ltd, company number 14105473. Our registered office is Units 15–17 Liberty House, Greenham Business Park, Greenham, Thatcham, England, RG19 6HS.

Information we use

Consultations and enquiries

When you book a meeting, we may receive your name, contact details, organisation, role, availability and information you provide about your product programme. We use this information to arrange the meeting, respond to your enquiry, prepare relevant technical and commercial discussions and manage any resulting business relationship.

We process this information where necessary to take steps at your request before entering a contract and, where applicable, for our legitimate interests in responding to business enquiries and developing customer relationships.

Service providers

Calendly and website hosting

Consultation buttons open Calendly in a new tab. Calendly processes booking information under its own privacy terms. Our website host may process routine technical request logs for security and reliable service operation. We do not currently use first-party website analytics or advertising trackers.

Retention

How long we keep information

We keep enquiry and meeting information only for as long as it is needed to handle your request, manage the relevant business relationship and meet legal, accounting or dispute-resolution requirements. Information that is no longer required is deleted or anonymised.

Your rights

Access and control

Depending on the circumstances, you may ask for access to your personal information, correction, deletion, restriction, portability or object to its use. You may also complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office.

To make a privacy request, write to Active-Edge Solutions Ltd at the registered office above. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.
