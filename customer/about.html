Our model

Leadership

Three disciplines. One product outcome.

Gary Turner

Electronic manufacture and commercial delivery

Gary has spent most of his career in electronic design and manufacture, bringing advanced products to market across automotive, aerospace, medical and defence applications. A co-founder of Active-PCB Solutions in 1997, he works from initial concept and design for manufacture through to testing and volume production.

Michael Hull

Electronic hardware engineering

Michael has more than 35 years of hardware experience across military, industrial and commercial products. A Loughborough University engineering graduate, he specialises in ARM-based embedded systems, system architecture, schematic and PCB design, communications, certification, evaluation and international production support.

Alex Lennon

Embedded software and connected systems

Alex has more than 25 years of embedded-software experience and founded Dynamic Devices in 2004. A Cambridge Electronic and Information Sciences graduate, he works across embedded Linux, Yocto/OpenEmbedded, real-time systems, Android, device drivers and resilient wired, radio, cellular and satellite connectivity.
